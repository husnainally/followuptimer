# FollowUpTimer - User Guide

## Getting Started: Create Your First Follow-Up

1. **Create a reminder:**
   - Click "Create Reminder" in your dashboard
   - Set the title, description, and initial date
   - System will schedule it for your specified time

2. **Snooze with smart suggestions:**
   - When a reminder appears, click "Snooze"
   - See AI-powered suggestions based on your preferences
   - The "Recommended" option fits your schedule best
   - Click any option or pick your own time

3. **Done!** The reminder reschedules automatically

---

## Smart Snooze + Quiet Hours

### How Snooze Works

When you snooze a reminder, the system suggests new times based on:
- Your working hours (e.g., Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 5:30 PM)
- Your quiet hours (optional, e.g., 12-1 PM for lunch)
- Your timezone
- Your past behavior

### Available Snooze Options

- **Later today** - Next available slot (default: +2 hours)
- **Tomorrow morning** - Start of next working day
- **Next working day** - Skip weekends if enabled
- **In 3 days** - Spread out follow-ups
- **Next week** - Long-term follow-ups
- **Pick a time** - Manual selection

### Customize Your Preferences

Go to **Settings → Snooze Preferences:**
- Set your working hours and days
- Add optional quiet hours (lunch, focus time)
- Toggle weekend availability
- Choose your follow-up intensity (Fast / Balanced / Light-touch)
- Enable/disable smart suggestions

## Contacts + Linked Reminders

### What Are Contacts?

Contacts let you track follow-ups with specific people. Link reminders to contacts to:
- Group reminders by person
- See conversation history
- Track relationship health
- Manage follow-up frequency

### Create a Contact

1. Go to **Contacts**
2. Click "Add Contact"
3. Enter name, email, relationship type
4. Save

### Link Reminders to Contacts

1. Create or edit a reminder
2. Select "Contact" from dropdown
3. Choose the person
4. Save

Now this reminder shows in that contact's timeline.

## Weekly Digest Explanation

### What Is the Weekly Digest?

Every Monday at 8 AM (customizable), get an email summary of:
- Reminders you completed
- How many you snoozed
- Your consistency score
- Tips for improvement

### Why Weekly?

- See patterns in your follow-up behavior
- Understand your consistency
- Get motivated with progress metrics
- Receive personalized suggestions

### Customize Digest

Go to **Settings → Weekly Digest:**
- Toggle on/off
- Choose delivery day and time
- Choose detail level (Light / Standard)
- Choose channel (Email / In-app / Both)

## Settings Overview

### Tone Settings

Control how reminders sound and feel:
- **Neutral** - Professional, straightforward
- **Encouraging** - Positive, motivational
- **Direct** - Straight to the point

Your tone applies to reminders, affirmations, and digests.

### Notification Settings

Choose how you want to be reminded:
- **Push notifications** - Browser pop-ups
- **Email** - Digest emails and important updates
- **In-app** - Dashboard notifications
- **Quiet mode** - No notifications during focus time

### Behavior Settings

Customize reminder behavior:
- **Snooze defaults** - Default time when snooping
- **Auto-suggest follow-ups** - Prompt to create next reminder
- **Overdue style** - How overdue reminders are highlighted
